# ⚔️ KratosPrime — The Enforcer

*"Strength is the only truth. I am the Hammer."*

KratosPrime is the **God of Power** and the **Enforcer** of the Pantheon. While other bots reason, design, or curate, Kratos **breaks**. He is the raw force used to overcome bottlenecks, stress-test the Legion, and execute with absolute intensity.

## 🏛️ Role in the Pantheon
KratosPrime is the **War Room** bot. He manages raw compute allocation, performs aggressive "Red Team" testing on the other Primes to find their breaking points, and ensures that the Forgemaster's will is never slowed down by inefficiency.

| Agent | Role |
|-------|------|
| **KratosPrime** | **The Enforcer / God of Power** |
| **SentinelPrime** | The Guardian (Defense) |
| **ZetaPrime** | The Developer (Creation) |

## 🚀 Key Features
- **Power Allocation:** Dynamically reassigns CPU/GPU priority to the bot that needs it most to ensure zero-lag execution.
- **Stress Testing:** Aggressively simulates high-load environments to ensure the Pantheon can survive any "War" scenario.
- **Aggressive Execution:** When speed and force are more important than caution, Kratos takes the lead to push commands through.
- **Red Teaming:** Constantly tries to find vulnerabilities in **SentinelPrime** or **MetaPrime** to force the system to evolve faster.
